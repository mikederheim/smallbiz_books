SmallBiz Books update: invoice payment lock

What this fixes:
- Once a payment is recorded against an invoice, the invoice can no longer be edited.
- Once a payment is recorded, the invoice payment form is hidden and duplicate payments are blocked server-side.
- Deleting an invoice still deletes the invoice, its payment records, and related ledger postings.

Install:
1. Back up your current smallbiz_books folder and MySQL database.
2. Copy the app folder in this update over your existing app folder.
3. No MySQL changes are required. The SQL file is included only as a note.

Changed files:
- app/Controllers/InvoiceController.php
- app/Views/invoices/index.php
- database/upgrade_2026_05_invoice_payment_lock.sql
